E-commerce

We help you to provide an efficient and accessible distribution channels for your products or services via an online marketplace, allowing consumers to complete sales transactions.

The technical solution provided by e-company to implement an e-commerce platform is based on the study of e-commerce and the development of integrated e-commerce to achieve real value for the market due to the large number of people who adopt trade and the degree of credibility, working under trade The e-commerce business platform has two applications :

1-Admin application and through it, site managers can control all the contents of the site and include:

Managing the main and sub-categories of products

Managing customers, offers and gifts for customers

Managing and following up purchase orders

Managing product prices and specifications

Managing inventory and returns

Managing shipping companies and following up the movement of shipping products

Managing and following up on electronic payment payments to customers and electronic wallets. Payment collection

Daily and statistical reports for products and customers, payments and returns - Database backups

2-Website and mobile application for customers

Registration page to log in to the website

Manage personal and electronic payment data for customers

Product search pages by brand, category, type and use

Shopping cart page

Product search and product evaluation pages

Featured products, new gifts and offers

Interaction page with managers, website, support service, conversations with service provider, social media pages, and geo-identification system
